Diploma

Universiti Teknoloogi Mara (UiTM) Kedah

UiTM Kedah, where I pursued my diploma, is a prestigious institution known for its commitment to excellence in education. The campus in Kedah offers a conducive learning environment with modern facilities and a dedicated faculty that supports student success. Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) was established in 1956 and has since grown to become one of Malaysia's largest and most renowned universities. With a focus on providing affordable and quality education, UiTM has played a significant role in shaping the nation's skilled workforce and contributing to its development. Studying at UiTM Kedah has been a rewarding experience, allowing me to acquire valuable knowledge and skills that have prepared me for the future.
